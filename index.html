Pineapple Cherry Cake

Classic meal while coding html like there is no tomorrow

Ingredients

  • 1 can (20 ounces) crushed pineapple, undrained
  • 1 can (21 ounces) cherry or blueberry pie filling
  • 1 package yellow cake mix (regular size)
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Evenly sprinkle pineapple in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish. Carefully spread with pie filling. Sprinkle with dry cake mix and drizzle with butter.
  2. Bake until top is golden brown, 50-60 minutes.
  3. Eat it after cooling down!
